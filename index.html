Have you ever wanted to eat breakfast in Paris, lunch in Hong Kong, and dinner in the Andromeda galaxy? Well now you can. Lytefast provides you with nearly instantaneous travel to any destination in the known universe. The days of sitting in traffic are soon to be a thing of the past as personal Lytefast transportation pads become available to the public. Utilizing advances in quantum physics and wormhole technology, Lytefast allows teleportation to take place with a nearly 0% chance of your DNA becoming scrambled resulting in unfortunate mutations.

How does it work?

To achieve instantaneous teleportation, Lytefast only needs a regular power outlet and a connection to the Lytefast wormhole servers. Simply step on to the Lytefast transportation pad with any belongings you wish to take on your travels, enter in coordinates for your destination, and press the big red button. The Lytefast servers will determine the best wormhole to accomodate your transportation needs, tear a hole in the fabric of space-time and away you go.

FAQ

What are the side effects of Lytefast transportation?
Lytefast transportation is almost 100% safe and painless. In rare cases, the stress of being ripped through a wormhole at speeds faster than light can cause genetic mutations which can lead to interesting side effects including but not limited to: sudden death, explosion, implosion, extra fingers, extra toes, extra arms, extra legs, ability to communicate with animals, and glowing green skin. Lytefast is not responsible for any side effects that you may encounter. The Lytefast transportation pad should be used at your own risk.
How do I return from my destination?
After you are done with your travel, the return process is just as simple as the journey there. Simply return to the location where you materialized through the wormhole, click your heels a couple of times, and hop back into the wormhole. If for any reason your wormhole has closed, try to find a way to start a signal fire and hope for a speedy rescue.
